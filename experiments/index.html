About Me

I'm Brandon Lin, a freshman at the University of Pennsylvania's College of Arts and Sciences

Before coming to Penn, I went to Stuyvesant High School, a school located in downtown Manhattan (New York City). While there, my main academic interests have been grounded in the hard sciences, mathematics, and computer science.

Some of my other interests include speedcubing, coding, and music.
